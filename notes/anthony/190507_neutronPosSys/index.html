Position Systematic for the PN Neutron Spectra

05/07/19

A.N. Villano

Abstract:

It is a long-standing worry that the position of the neutron source for the Photoneutron running was not stable. It was shown that the total rates from the detectors should be constant (modulo source decay) within about 10% [Vil18-PN62], but the systematic difference in the shape of the neutron spectrum was not investigated. Here we show that the shape differences of the neutron spectra are for most recoil energies and source configurations below about 4% and everywhere below 10%.
